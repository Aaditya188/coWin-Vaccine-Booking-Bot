# coWin Vaccine Booking Bot
Easy Vaccine Booking Facility During this Pandemic 

# Submission by Team NEXUS for HackACode 2021

<h2 align= "left"><b>Our Product</b></h2>

We NEXUS present before you,<b>"coWin Vaccine Booking Bot"</b>.

<img width=40% src="Screenshots/logo.PNG"> &ensp;


## Our Idea
No more waiting hours to find a slot for vaccination. With the help of our CoWin bot, you can fill in the details and wait for the bot to find free slot on its own.

## Problems we Faced :
We faced many problems in importing the API and launching the webpage on our domain. It took a while but we finlly achieved our goal.
